Pharmacist - Boots Pharmacy - Ivybridge

This is the main service - What does that mean?

In the DoS, every service in your pharmacy has its own opening times. To make it easier to change them, a 'main service' has been chosen for each pharmacy.
You can choose whether the other services should match the 'main service' or have different opening times.

Normally, the 'main service' will be your core pharmacist service. Don't worry if the wrong service has been chosen - the DoS will not be affected, so it does not matter.
You can still update your services so that each one has the correct opening times.
