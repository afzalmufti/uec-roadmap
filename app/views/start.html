Pharmacy DoS Quality Payments

Use this service to check and update Directory of Service (DoS) records for your pharmacy.

You can either:

  • confirm that the details are correct
  • tell us what needs to be changed

You will need to know:

  • the ODS (F) code for your pharmacy
  • the address, postcode and telephone number
  • the opening times for each of your pharmacy services

Many pharmacies will have multiple records on the DoS and you need to check all of them.
