Prototype of a region-level 'location profile' page, with a single layout showing images, region overview, data points presented as an infographic and an interactive map.

Interactive map allows user to see what industries are active in a region, what transport links are there, as well as R&D Hubs, financial incentives, etc.

Contact form

Fairly straight reworking of the contact form on one page, but removed the 123 from desktop (already supressed on live for mobile)

One thing per page - this is the recommended GDS model for simple and complex forms, not literally one thing, but multi-page simple forms perform better than over-grouping of elements on fewer pages.
