St Pancras Hospital, 4 ST PANCRAS WAY, LONDON, NW1 0PE

REFERRAL CRITERIA:
Service is available to registered and unregistered patients residing within the London boroughs of Barnet, Enfield, Haringey, Camden and Islington.

Patients not residing within north central London.
