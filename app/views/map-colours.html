Colours

I picked a couple of fairly neutral colour palettes to use for our data. The heatmap one uses both brightness + colour to display a range of values, so will work without colour vision.

On the heatmap dotmap, I put a copy of the UK dots behind and offset, half a dot up+right. This copy is 85% opacity of the main blobs. Or use the values above.
