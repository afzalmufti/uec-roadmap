Important Information

Please advise the patient to call the Pharmacy before attending and:

  • Ask to speak to the PHARMACIST about an “Emergency Supply” of your medicine
  • The pharmacist will need to be confident that you are regularly prescribed this medicine. You can show this by
    • Giving permission for them to access your Summary Care Record or speak to your usual pharmacy
    • Sometimes taking a repeat slip or empty pack can help
  • NHS Prescription Charges apply. The medicine will be free if you are exempt from NHS charges
